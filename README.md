# AI DecodeX Hackathon Project

## 🚀 Overview
AI DecodeX is an AI-powered academic intelligence platform designed to transform past examination papers into strategic study insights.

It helps students analyze multiple previous year papers, identify high-frequency topics, detect syllabus gaps, predict exam-important concepts, and generate personalized smart study plans.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Students often spend hours manually reviewing previous year question papers without clear insights into:
- Frequently repeated topics
- Important question patterns
- Syllabus coverage gaps
- High-priority preparation strategy

---

## 💡 Our Solution
AI DecodeX automates this process by providing:
- 📂 Multi-paper upload support
- 📊 Predictive exam weightage analysis
- ⚠️ Syllabus risk zone detection
- 🧠 Personalized smart study planner
- 🎯 High ROI practice recommendations
- 📈 Visual academic intelligence dashboard

---

## 🔥 Key Features
### 1. Multi-Paper Upload
Upload previous year papers in PDF format.

### 2. AI Pattern Analysis
Identifies recurring topics and exam trends.

### 3. Predictive Topic Scoring
Ranks subjects/topics based on historical frequency.

### 4. Syllabus Coverage Gap Detection
Highlights underrepresented but critical syllabus zones.

### 5. Smart Study Planner
Generates a prioritized day-wise preparation roadmap.

### 6. Practice Question Recommendations
Suggests high-value practice focus areas.

---

## 🌍 Impact
AI DecodeX reduces inefficient exam preparation by converting raw historical papers into actionable academic strategy, saving time and improving focus.

---

## 🧠 Innovation
Unlike traditional paper review systems, AI DecodeX combines paper intelligence, predictive scoring, syllabus analysis, and strategic planning into one academic decision-support platform.
